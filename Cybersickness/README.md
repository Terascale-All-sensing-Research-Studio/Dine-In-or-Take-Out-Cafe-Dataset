# Virtual Reality Sickness Questionnaire (VRSQ)

The standard Virtual Reality Sickness Questionnaire (VRSQ) was administered at the end of the study [[1]](#1). The VRSQ asks participants to rate their state across the following dimensions:

* General Discomfort
* Fatigue
* Headache
* Eye Strain
* Difficulty Focusing
* Fullness of the Head
* Blurred Vision
* Dizziness with Eyes Closed
* Vertigo

VRSQ responses are provided in two CSV file formats: Numerical and String

| Dataset Column | Question | Response Value | 
| ------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| 1 | Participant ID | Unique 5-character ID assigned to each participant |
| 2 | General Discomfort | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 3 | Fatigue | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 4 | Headache | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 5 | Eye Strain | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 6 | Difficulty Focusing | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 7 | Fullness of the Head | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 8 | Blurred Vision | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 9 | Dizziness with Eyes Closed | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
| 10 | Vertigo | 1 being None; 2 being Slight; 3 being Moderate; 4 being Severe |
